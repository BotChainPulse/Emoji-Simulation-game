## 🕹️ How to Play

1. **Select a Character** from the top panel (Cheater, Friendly, or Hostile)
2. **Click anywhere** on the blue game area to place your character
3. **Drag characters** to move them around
4. **Double-click** a character to remove it
5. **Press Delete/Backspace** to clear all characters
